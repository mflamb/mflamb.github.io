Mike Lamb crazy about growth

Hi, my name is Mike. I'm a web developer with a natural inclination to solve complex problems.

I'm curious about the kinds of things that most people find inaccessible and forbidding.

I'm the kind of guy who goes into Postgres manually and starts teaching himself SQL commands for fun.

I'm the guy who fell in love with Git and the Command Line from day one.

I thrive on a formidable challenge. I strive to continually seek out greater ones in my professional endeavors because I love the growth that comes under scary conditions.

I discovered a love of coding completely by accident. At my last job as a Navy SAP Support Contractor, I was taking Excel tutorials to level up my skills when I got exposed to writing macros with VBA. Having been an English major in college, I was always fascinated by the structure of language and how subtle changes to it can have such vastly different impacts.

Over the next six months my interest grew increasingly serious, and I took tutorials ranging from the fundamentals of "Logic and Computational Thinking" all the way to Ruby and JavaScript beginner courses.

Since that time, I've been committed to dedicating my professional life to the pursuit of developing and perfecting a true craft.

searchCloud

View Live GitHub Repo

A searchable custom jukebox powered by Soundcloud's REST API. Users can search by keyword for songs, play a song and display artist/track information with a click. It was designed so that song selection wouldn't depend on iterating through an array. This project has a special place in my heart because it was my breakthrough with writing clean JavaScript for the first time!
